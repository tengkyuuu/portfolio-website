Project 01: Eco-Friendly Rebrand

REF: PRJ-2023-01

FIG 1.1: Sustainable Visual Identity System and Material Explorations.

Challenge

The client, a leading supplier of organic agricultural products, suffered from a fragmented brand identity that failed to communicate their high-end market position. Existing materials were inconsistent across digital and print touchpoints, leading to a 15% decrease in brand recall among primary stakeholders.

Solution

Developed a comprehensive 'Document-First' design system utilizing a palette of earth-toned neutrals and high-contrast typography. We restructured their digital documentation flow and physical packaging to use sustainable materials, resulting in a cohesive narrative that increased stakeholder engagement by 34% within the first quarter.

Project 02: FinTech Mobile App

REF: PRJ-2023-04

FIG 2.1: Transactional Flow Architecture and Data Visualization Modules.

Metric Pre-Launch Post-Launch Growth
User Retention 42% 78% +85.7%
Load Time (Avg) 3.4s 0.8s -76.4%

Challenge

The legacy banking application suffered from high churn rates due to a complex navigation structure and outdated visual language. Mobile users reported difficulty in accessing core transactional features, leading to an average task completion time of over 120 seconds for basic transfers.

Solution

Implemented a modular 'Bento-style' dashboard that prioritized high-frequency actions. By applying Material Design principles focused on information hierarchy and reducing cognitive load, the new architecture reduced task completion time to 24 seconds and stabilized user retention.
